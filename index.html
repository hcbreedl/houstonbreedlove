UNC Chapel Hill Coding Boot Camp
Schedulr

Schedulr was the project I was most proud of coming out of the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Boot Camp. Why? Because this was the successful completion of a MERN stack application. We accomplished our goal of delivering what we as a group, agreed to achieve within 2 weeks.

The idea of Schedulr was to begin coming up with a way for a manager to manage scheduling for his/her employees.

Tech stack: Mongo, Mongoose.js, Express.js, React, React Router, Node.js, Passport.js
